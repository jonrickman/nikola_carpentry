Our Mission

The mission of Nikola Custom Carpentry and Woodworking is to give every customer exactly what they want and to make it with such craftsmanship that it will stand the test of time and become family heirlooms. I start this process by meeting/talking with each customer and getting an idea of what the customer is wanting. During this meeting, I ask multiple questions to determine what type of materials will be needed, take/get measurements to know how much materials will be needed, and to get an overall understanding of what will be needed to complete the project. I then price out the materials so that I can give the customer an estimate for the deposit for the project. Once the deposit is given, I start working on the project, many times taking progression pictures of the project and sending them to the customer to make sure I am meeting their expectations. Once the project is complete, the customer can then choose to have their project sent to them, delivered if close enough, or to come pick it up themselves. When the customer receives their project, I ask them to inspect it closely to make sure it is what they requested. After the customer’s inspection, if there is anything they are not satisfied with, I simply request they send/give the project back so that I can fix it, free of charge.

Logistics

Nikola Custom Carpentry primarily services the greater Birmingham area and is based out of Morris, Alabama.
