The owner, Mark Nikola, is a husband and father of two beautiful children, Phillip and Gwendolyn. His father, Nick Nikola, has done carpentry and woodworking all his life. Starting at a young age, Mark would watch his dad as he would do projects in his workshop. Mark would help his dad by pulling nails out of boards and gathering up scrap pieces of wood. As he grew older, Mark’s dad would start letting him help with his projects by showing him how you cut pieces of wood with a tabletop jigsaw making jigsaw puzzles. Eventually, Mark was able to start helping his dad with bigger projects, like helping to build decks, working on additions to his dad’s workshop and their house, and many other various projects. Now, Mark has taken his love and creativity for the art of carpentry and woodworking to the next level by starting a small business, Nikola Custom Carpentry and Woodworking. Mark would just like to remind every customer that when you buy from a small business, you’re not only helping support a father and his family, you are also helping to support the goals and dreams of a wife and two children.
Mark has worked in multiple businesses that deal with customer service since he started his own business mowing lawns for six customers before the age of 14. That turned into working construction during his years in high school. After starting college, Mark started working in the retail industry and worked in it for over 15 years. That was when he truly realized how many times the customer never really received the service they truly deserved. Now, as a worker in the healthcare industry, Mark has learned that helping people has become a true calling to him.
Comment from the owner: “I started this business with the goals of giving EVERY customer EXACTLY what they want and to make it with such craftsmanship that it will stand the test of time and become family heirlooms. I have discovered that one of my passions in this life is to find a way that I can truly help people. After having worked in companies that dealt with customer service for over 15 years and having seen, firsthand, how many times they fail at delivering this, customer satisfaction will always be my top priority.”
